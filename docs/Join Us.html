Membership Requirements

  • Complete the membership application form.
  • Pay a non-refundable registration fee of KSh 200.
  • Contribute a minimum monthly savings of KSh 200.
  • Pay a non-refundable monthly Risk Fund of KSh 50.
  • Purchase share capital of KSh 3,000 (non-refundable but transferable).
  • Provide valid identification and proof of residence in Kilifi, Mombasa, or Kwale.
