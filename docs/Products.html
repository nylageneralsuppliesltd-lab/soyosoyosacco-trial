Loan Policy

Eligibility

  • Active membership for at least 6 months.
  • Minimum savings contribution of KSh 3,200.

Loan Limits

  • Borrow up to 3 times your savings, capped at KSh 1,000,000.
  • Loans approved by the management committee, exclusive to members.

Security

  • Secured by member or guarantor deposits.
  • Loans exceeding savings require guarantors.

Our Loan Products

Development Loan

Finance property acquisition, business startups, or agricultural projects.

  • Up to KSh 1,000,000 (3x savings)
  • 1% monthly interest (simple)
  • 12-month repayment
  • Requires 3 guarantors

Emergency Loan

Quick financial support for unexpected challenges.

  • 3% monthly interest (simple)
  • 3-month repayment
  • One loan at a time
  • Requires 1 guarantor

Medicare Loan

Cover medical expenses with direct payments to hospitals.

  • 4% annual interest (0.33% monthly, simple)
  • 12-month repayment
  • Amount based on repayment ability
  • Requires 3 guarantors

Education Loan

Fund school fees with direct payments to educational institutions.

  • 4% annual interest (0.33% monthly, simple)
  • 12-month repayment
  • Amount based on repayment ability
  • Requires 3 guarantors
